What We Do

TSI Group acts as an international sourcing company that seeks to develop and distribute a wide array of consumer electronics across emerging markets in Southeast Asian region. Our product line spans from televisions, laptops, home audio and theater, gadgets and accessories, sports watches, and more. We accommodate products not only for consumers but also for vertical end-to-end markets seeking unique customization and additional innovative features that are not yet commercially available to fulfill their industry based requirement and standards.

As a one-stop destination, our mission is to be the premiere innovative all-in-one distributor of the highest quality and widest array of consumer electronic products and offering alternative product and brand selections with competitive and affordable prices for Filipinos to support what really matters in their lives. We are always targeting in introducing new product developments first in the market. Our matchless service is truly exceptional. We assure you that we are an asset to your team.

What Set Us Apart

Our vision is to be the country’s leading and front-runner in consumer electronics distribution with the largest product offering in both retail and vertical markets. As a consumer electronics distributor, we have extensive product knowledge on the latest cutting-edge technologies in the electronic industry surpassing our customers’ expectations where we build connections and relationships that lasts, and be your “Single Sourcing Company. ” Our professional innovative thinkers and developers conduct extensive research on today’s technology and products so retailers can have the widest and advanced array of gadgets and accessories for them to meet their customers’ needs and wants. Our team of professionals is always ready to find the best solutions for you. As there will be an unceasing need for electronic products and services, Technostore will persistently strive to be the best and top responsible class distributor of quality products and services.
