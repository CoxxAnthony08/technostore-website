PRIVACY POLICY

TSI Group International and Technostore Incorporated and its affiliated companies are dedicated to protect and respect your privacy. We aim to follow all applicable laws on privacy protection and personal information security. We respect the privacy of our customers and online visitors. The purpose of our Privacy Policy is to inform you about our online information practices concerning the collection, processing, and use of personal information.



COLLECTION OF INFORMATION

We collect and use different kinds of information that will help us perform and serve well our customer and visitors to our website:

  • Internet Protocol (IP) Addresses. For every access and browse on our website, our web server automatically identifies and saves the visitor’s IP address to know which areas of our website are being visited and the time each visitor spends there. Having this information will help us understand traffic patterns through our website so that we can fix and further improve the quality of the overall usability of our website.
  • Personal Information. As we do not generally require you to provide us with your information to visit or access our website, we may definitely ask you to provide us with your personal information associated when you contact us for queries with your identifiable information such as your e-mail address, name, telephone or mobile phone number, feedback. Allowing us to have this information allows you to subscribe to newsletters and give us your feedback regarding our products and services. We do not require you to provide us with sensitive personal information (such as information about your political or religious beliefs, sexual orientation, and health). Any information you provide will only be used in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
